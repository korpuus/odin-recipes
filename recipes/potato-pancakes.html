Potato pancakes

These potato pancakes are wonderfully crispy and tasty

Crispy on the outside and smooth and creamy inside. Incredibly easy to make will make for ultimate comfort food!

Ingredients

  • Mashed potatoes
  • All-purpose flour
  • Onion
  • Egg
  • Salt and pepper
  • Vegetable Oil

Steps

  1. Mix the mashed potatoes, flour, minced onion, egg, salt, and pepper. They should form a thick and creamy batter when done.
  2. Preheat some vegetable oil in a skillet over medium heat
  3. Drop a 4-inch mound of batter onto the preheated oil and skillet. Flatten it with a spatula and cook both sides for about 5 minutes each or until golden brown.
  4. Serve with sour cream and chives
