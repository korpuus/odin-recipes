Spaghetti and Meatballs

This is original italian recipe for Spaghetti and meatballs.

It's very easy to prepare and it's delicious dish, that will make anyone happy.

All you have to do is knead the meatballs, make the sauce, add the pasta and that's it!

Ingredients

Meatballs

  • 6 ounces pork (ground)
  • 6 ounces beef (ground)
  • 1egg (whole)
  • garlic (1 clove)
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 spoon milk
  • 1 and 3/4 cup cheese (grated parmesan)
  • 1 and 3/4 cup bread crumb
  • 1 and 1/2 cup olive oil
  • 1 and 1/4 cup parsley (minced)
  • black pepper (ground )

Sauce

  • 8 ounces tomato sauce (1 can)
  • garlic (1 clove)
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • 1 spoon olive oil
  • 14 ounces spaghetti
  • 1onion (minced)
  • spoons salt (1 and 1/2 spoon)
  • 10 basil (chopped fresh leaves)

Steps

Meatballs

  1. In a bowl combine the meat, Parmesan, eggs, milk, breadcrumbs, parsley, garlic, salt and pepper.
  2. Mix using your hands.
  3. Prepare about 25/30 1 inch balls.
  4. Arrange them on a baking sheet.
  5. Heat the olive oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat.
  6. Add the meatballs (not all together) and brown on all sides for 5 minutes.
  7. Drain on a paper towel lined plate. Set the meatballs aside and keep the cooking oil in the pan.

Sauce

  1. In the same pan where they cooked the meatballs add the onion and garlic and cook until they become translucent.
  2. Add the tomato sauce, chopped basil leaves, salt, sugar, ground pepper if used.
  3. Stir and cook over medium / low heat for about 20 minutes.
  4. In the meantime, cook the spaghetti in a pot with boiling salted water.

Final

  1. Add the meatballs to the pan with the sauce and mix gently.
  2. Lower the heat to low and cook for another 15 minutes.
  3. Drain the spaghetti al dente and pour them into the pan with the sauce and meatballs.
  4. Mix everything together and cook for another minute.

That's it! Serve the spaghetti with the meatballs and sprinkle with more Parmesan.
